Jesus Calms the Storms

(Mark 4:34-5:24)

After teaching to the people, Jesus and his disciples went in a boat and travelled. Jesus fell asleep in the back of the boat and the sea was calm. Suddenly a storm comes in and makes giant waves. The disciples were scared that they would die in the storm. The disciples went to wake Jesus up. Jesus woke up and got up and told the sea to be still. And the sea was still, then Jesus asked his disciples why they were afraid. The disciples were so amazed at what Jesus did.
