The Burning Bush

(Exodus 3-4:1-17)

Exodus 3-4:1-17 talks about how God talks to Moses through a burning bush that did not burn up. When God asks Moses to come and save his people from the Egyptians. Moses was doubting that he was not the right person for the job and every time God said that he will be with him Moses kept doubting so at the end God sent Moses brother Aaron to go with him to set the Hebrews free from the Egyptians.
