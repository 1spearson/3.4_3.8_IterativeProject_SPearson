Jesus Tempted

(Matthew 4:1-11)

After Jesus is baptized the holy spirit leads him to the desert. For 40 days and 40 nights he was there with no food. During the time the devil tempted him 3 times and every time Jesus said scripture against the devil and every time he got angry after the 3rd temptation the devil left Jesus and angels came and looked after him.

Bible Study Questions

  • What have you learned about Jesus/God
  • What is God telling you to think about from the story?
  • Are you being tempted by the enemy? If so, how can you resist the temptation?
  • What can you do to go closer to God?
