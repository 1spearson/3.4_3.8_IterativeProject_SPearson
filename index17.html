Easter

Summary


Easter starts on Good Friday where Jesus was tried and put to death on a cross for all of us, he died with all of our sins and put in a tomb. Then on Sunday we celebrate that Jesus is alive as he rose up from the grave so we can come to him, and so we have a way to go to heaven after we have finished the plan God has for us.
