Palm Sunday

Summary


Palm Sunday is the Sunday before Easter. Palm Sunday is when we celebrate Jesus coming to Jerusalem as king as he is riding the donkey. The people were shouting hosanna and declaring Jesus as king.  We celebrate Palm Sunday by going to church and resting on the day.
