Noah’s Ark

(Genesis 6-9:17)

Genesis 6 talks about the start of the story of the world going wrong and being corrupted by sin, and introduces Noah and the plan God has for Noah on building and loading animals, 1 male and 1 female. Genesis 7 talks about the whole earth being covered by the flood, and the only survivors Genesis 8 talks about how God remembers Noah and how Noah finds dry land and for months has been waiting, but God did not forget Noah. After the flood was gone, Noah made an altar and praised the lord. In Genesis 9:1 -17 talk about God's promises to Noah and he puts a rainbow in the sky
