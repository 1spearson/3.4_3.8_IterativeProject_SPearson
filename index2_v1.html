Facts on the Bible

  • There are 2 different kind of numbers the big numbers is called chapters and the small numbers are called verses.

  • You can get a bible from the op shop, Mana Christian Bookshop in Lower Hutt, or you can download the Youversion bible app on you phone for free.

  • The Bible is written by people thousands of  years again on God and what he did in their life.

  • The bible also has songs and poems written by King David in Psalms.

  • God can speak to you while you are reading the bible. It's important to know what stands out when reading the bible.

  • Don’t read the bible like a book read a little at a time and reflect on what you are reading
