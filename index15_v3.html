Christmas

Summary


Christmas as you know is a holiday for presents, going on a vacation and spending time with family, but all that is not the reason why we celebrate Christmas. We as Christians still do that stuff on Christmas day, but we celebrate the birth of the saviour of the world Jesus Christ who is the good news in your life and everyone else. Over 4 weeks before Christmas, we prepare our hearts for the coming king, which is called advent. We light the candles and remember what Christmas is all about. We light the hope candle on the first week, then on the second Sunday we light the peace candle, then the joy then the love candle.  On Christmas day, there is a Christmas service at Tawa College Hall
